As you may have seen, Tally Hall’s sophomore record ‘Good & Evil’ is being re-pressed by Needlejuice Records. To coincide with the release, they are also releasing a companion 7″ vinyl record single consisting of ‘Turn The Lights Off’, and, for the first time ever on a physical format: ‘Light & Night’. This new release(!!) appropriately, has brand new artwork by Drew Mokris, who was the director and animator for the Turn The Lights Off music video
